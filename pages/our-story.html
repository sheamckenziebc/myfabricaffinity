Our Story

This summer pop-up is stocked full of goodies from our last atelier collection.

We are Amanda & a small team of talented helpers, who thankfully make MFA studio processes a fun & passionate supporter of local makers & creativity.

We originally brought these cherished textiles & threads to Victoria with plans to start making more few-of-a-kind fashions. Soon after the move from Ontario, Amanda fell ill & life had to pause for over 4 years.

Now, as Amanda heals & reconnects with her true calling - songwriting & singing - she’s choosing to step away from the fashion industry.

This summer pop-up is more than just a sale, it’s to support her rebirth; it is a farewell & a celebration of everything we’ve built together..

We’ve always had a deep love for fabric & understand the powerful wonders, workings & needs of a fashion maker. Amanda brings over 40 years experience with textile & threads, beginning her work in fashion even before college studies in technique & design. With many generations of fashion design & construction in her family, pursuing this path felt natural. She specialized in custom design, with work spanning wardrobe & image consulting, costume design for film & music videos, visual merchandising & photo shoot styling, & even interiors. Though highly skilled in illustration, pattern drafting & sewing, her most recent years were dedicated to teaching & consulting with fellow makers & designers. Thank you for being part of this chapter.

How We Roll

Amanda & MFA are committed to helping you bring your textile projects to life. With joy, we present these carefully selected treasures - intentionally & primarily to local designers & makers. Reducing the need for shipping is one way we aim to keep things as eco-friendly as possible. Most of these textiles are natural fibers, threads made in Canada & come with minimal packaging. For those in Victoria & surrounding areas, hand delivery is available at no extra cost. We’re also happy to arrange shipping if needed.

We’re proud to offer exceptional customer service, to support & encourage your creative process. We wish for these beautiful, quality textiles & threads to bring you as much joy & inspiration as they brought us.

You are invited to text, call or email Amanda to place an order or book a private shopping sesion at our Metchosin studio.

Garden breaks are included.

Here’s to making yummy futures real.
